About Get Your Garage Back

Get Your Garage Back was founded by Jordan right here in Austin, Texas. After building Hill Country Cleaning Co. into a five-star rated cleaning company serving the greater Austin area — Jordan kept seeing the same problem in garage after garage: homeowners who had given up on ever getting their space back.

We are not a junk removal company that dumps and leaves. We sort with you, organize what stays, haul what goes, and hand you back a clean, usable garage. Every job is flat-rate with no hidden fees. If we are not done in one day, you don't pay.
