Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 7, 2026

Get Your Garage Back LLC ("we," "us," or "our") provides one-day garage cleanout services in the south and southwest Austin, Texas area. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect when you request a quote through our website or Facebook/Instagram lead form, how we use it, and the choices you have.

Information we collect

When you fill out our quote form on getyourgarageback.co or through a Facebook or Instagram lead ad, we collect:

  • Your name
  • Your phone number
  • Your email address
  • Your zip code
  • Your answers to our quote questions (type of garage, condition, timeline)
  • Any photos or messages you send us when following up about your quote

We do not collect sensitive personal information, financial information, or government-issued identifiers.

How we use your information

We use the information you give us to:

  • Send you a flat-rate quote by text message, usually within 15 minutes
  • Follow up by text, email, or phone about your quote and schedule your job if you book
  • Confirm appointment details and share job-day logistics
  • Improve our service quality and pricing accuracy over time
  • Measure the effectiveness of our advertising so we can show ads to the right people

Who we share information with

We share your information only with the service providers we need to run our business:

  • Text messaging provider -- to deliver your quote and appointment messages.
  • Meta (Facebook and Instagram) -- when you submit a lead through one of our ads, Meta receives your submission as part of delivering the ad. We also send aggregated and hashed conversion data back to Meta via its Conversions API so we can measure which ads work and improve targeting. This is standard for businesses that advertise on Facebook and Instagram.
  • Scheduling and field-service software -- to schedule your job and manage the crew on job day.
  • Payment processor -- if you choose to pay electronically, we pass your payment details to our payment processor. We never store your full card number.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to anyone. We do not share your information with unrelated marketers or data brokers.

Text Messaging (SMS/MMS) Privacy

When you provide your phone number and consent to receive text messages from Get Your Garage Back LLC, we may send you text messages related to your garage cleanout inquiries, quotes, appointments, scheduling updates, and customer support.

No mobile information will be shared with third parties/affiliates for marketing/promotional purposes. All other categories exclude text messaging originator opt-in data and consent; this information will not be shared with any third parties.

We do not sell, rent, or share your phone number or text messaging opt-in consent with any third parties for their marketing purposes. Text messaging originator opt-in data and consent will not be shared with any third parties, except as necessary for the delivery of text messages through our messaging service provider.

Message Frequency: Message frequency varies based on your interactions with us. You may receive multiple messages related to a single service inquiry or appointment.

Opt-Out: You may opt out of receiving text messages at any time by replying STOP to any text message from us. You may also text STOP, UNSUBSCRIBE, CANCEL, END, or QUIT to opt out. After opting out, you will receive a confirmation message and no further text messages will be sent. You may also opt out by contacting us at the email or phone number listed below.

Help: For help or questions about our text messaging program, reply HELP to any text message, email us at hello@getyourgarageback.co, or call (512) 877-3275.

Message and Data Rates: Message and data rates may apply. Check with your mobile carrier for details about your messaging plan.

Carrier Disclaimer: Carriers (T-Mobile, AT&T, Verizon, and others) are not liable for delayed or undelivered messages.

Delivery: Message delivery is subject to your mobile carrier's network availability and is not guaranteed. We are not responsible for messages that are not delivered due to carrier issues or device incompatibility.

How long we keep your information

We keep the information you give us for as long as we need it to serve you and meet our legal and tax obligations. If you ask us to delete your information and we have no legal obligation to keep it, we will.

Your rights

You can ask us at any time to:

  • See what information we have about you
  • Correct anything that is wrong
  • Delete your information from our systems
  • Stop contacting you

To make any of these requests, email us at hello@getyourgarageback.co.

Children

Our service is for homeowners and is not directed at children. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 13.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The effective date at the top of the page shows when it was last changed. If we make a meaningful change, we will update the date and, if appropriate, let our customers know directly.
